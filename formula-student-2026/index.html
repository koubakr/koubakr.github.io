Formula Student 2026

Project is still ongoing.

As the Head of Electronic Systems for the Swansea University Race Engineering (SURE) team for the 2026 Formula Student UK competition, I am responsible for the design and implementation of the vehicle's car electronics systems. My primary focus this season is integrating a new ECU as well as design and manufacturing of a new loom. We are also focusing on more sensors on the car to collect more data while testing, as well as refining our drive by wire and flat shifting system. We also aim to make a custom fuse box and implement a new dashboard and steering wheel system.
