Intelligent building installation

Loxone Smart Home System: A fully integrated automation project featuring three distribution boards, custom logic programming, and control of HVAC, lighting, and security systems.

In this renovation project, I designed and implemented a Loxone smart home system. The system architecture was developed in collaboration with a licensed electrician and a certified Loxone installer. Following the design phase, I executed the physical wiring, programming, system commissioning, and troubleshooting.

The infrastructure is structured in three distribution boards: a main unit serving the ground floor and two sub-distribution boards for the upper and lower levels. The project also features complex automation for a dedicated wellness room.

Programmed via Loxone Config, the system integrates:

  • Lighting and shading (blinds)
  • Underfloor heating and boiler temperature control
  • Presence detection and intrusion alarms 
  • Access control (Door locks, Intercom/Doorbell)
  • Wellness system controls (pool and sauna)

The home utilizes several logic modes to automate daily routines, to integrate into a persons life seamlessly:

  • Away Mode: Activates the alarm, closes blinds, turns off lights/motion detection, and lowers the temperature.
  • Sleep Mode: Modifies motion detection in bedrooms, dims lights, lowers temperature, closes blinds, and disables audio.
  • Lighting Scenes: Adapts internal brightness based on the sun's position and outdoor light intensity.
  • Overheating Protection: Automatically adjusts shading to block solar gain and prevent the rooms from overheating.
